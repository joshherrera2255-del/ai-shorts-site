Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. What We Collect

This application accesses only the TikTok account data required to upload and publish videos on behalf of the authenticated account owner: an access token and basic account identity. No other personal data is collected.

2. What We Do Not Do

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer any data to third parties. We do not track users. We do not collect data from viewers of published content.

3. Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the account owner's own computer and are never transmitted anywhere except to TikTok's official API.

4. Data Deletion

Revoke the app's access at any time in your TikTok settings, and delete the locally stored token file to remove all stored data.

5. Contact

For questions, contact the app owner directly.
